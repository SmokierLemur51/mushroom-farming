Malice Manor
CTF

Who, what, when, where and why?
Malice manor is a simulated Dark-Web marketplace set in the Harry Potter universe.

I have been teaching myself how to program over the last year or two, and I thought this project would be an interesting tribute to the knowledge and skillset I have devleoped. In the future, I would like to turn this into a CTF/Web-Dev learning center for those interested in learning outside of youtube tutorial hell.

I enjoy reading and learning about hacking, security best practices, methods of attack and web development. When I found out about the Tor Project, I became very interested in dark web marketplaces and their development process. I imagine building and operating a dark web marketplace is no easy feat, and after taking account of who your enemies are (black-hats, global law enforcement, and three letter agencies), you see how big of a feat it is to operate a market. So building my own marketplace (without the illegal content) seemed like a fun project to take on.
